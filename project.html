Why visit Lisbon?

Lisbon is a fascinating city full of history, monuments, impressive bridges and vintage street cars. And these are just a few of the reasons that make it an extremely interesting city to visit and a place you will want to return to. In 2016, Lisbon was ranked number 7 in the top ten European cities to visit. Its cheap flights, relative closeness to major capital cities like Madrid, Paris and London, make it an ideal destination for a weekend break. Visiting Lisbon is relatively inexpensive and you can find low-cost flights from the UK for just £30 ($36, 34€) and hotels from £55 ($78, 65€).
